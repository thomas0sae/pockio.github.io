Everything Your Family Needs

Powerful features designed to make money management fun and educational for kids

Virtual Money Jars

Create multiple savings jars for different goals - toys, games, charity, or college savings. Kids learn to allocate money wisely.

Chores Tracking

Assign chores with monetary rewards. Kids learn the value of hard work and earning their own money through completing tasks.

Savings Goals

Set visual savings goals with progress tracking. Watch your child's excitement as they get closer to their target!

Allowance Management

Schedule regular allowances and track spending history. Teach budgeting skills from an early age.

Parental Supervision

Full parental control over all activities. Approve transactions, set limits, and monitor your child's financial learning journey.

Progress Reports

Visual reports showing savings progress, chores completed, and financial habits developed over time.

Why Teach Kids About Money Early?

  • Build Lifelong Habits

    Children who learn money management early are more likely to be financially responsible adults.

  • Understand Value of Work

    Connecting chores to rewards teaches kids that money is earned through effort and responsibility.

  • Delayed Gratification

    Saving for goals teaches patience and the satisfaction of achieving something through persistence.

  • Family Bonding

    Work together as a family on financial goals and celebrate achievements together.
